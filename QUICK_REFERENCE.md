## Risk Management Rules

1. **Max Risk per Trade:** 1-2% of capital
2. **Minimum R:R Ratio:** 1:2
3. **Always use Stop-Loss**
4. **Position Size Formula:**
   ```
   Size = (Capital √ó Risk%) / (Entry - Stop)
   ```
